## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** bgdude's forecasts start a bit further out than the range useful for alerting. There's no short 15-minute-ahead forecast feeding the low-alert.

**Reason for change.** A calibrated 15-minute forecast can warn of a low earlier and more reliably than a simple threshold on the current reading — but only once the uncertainty band is trustworthy (the two prior tasks).
