## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** When sizing a correction dose, bgdude uses a fixed insulin-sensitivity number, even though the app separately computes that your sensitivity is currently higher or lower (for example after exercise).

**Reason for change.** Correcting with a static sensitivity while the app knows it's shifted can over- or under-dose. Feeding the known adjustment into the correction (bounded, and shown in the working) is safer.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
