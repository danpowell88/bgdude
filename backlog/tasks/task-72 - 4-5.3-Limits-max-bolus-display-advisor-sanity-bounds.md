## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** The pump knows its own safety limits — the maximum single bolus it will allow and the maximum basal rate. bgdude can read these but doesn't use them. These read messages were confirmed to return real data during the July 2026 live-pump exploration (the Protocol Explorer sweep); their exact byte layouts are recorded in doc/pump-protocol.md.

**Reason for change.** Beyond just displaying them, bgdude should never suggest a bolus larger than the pump's own configured maximum — a small but real safety guard on the advisor's output.
