## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** bgdude's alert thresholds are a single value used all day. But the right threshold differs by time — you may want an earlier warning overnight than during a busy afternoon.

**Reason for change.** Per-time-of-day thresholds (overnight / day / post-meal) catch overnight lows and post-meal highs without nagging during the day. Existing values simply become the "all day" row.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
