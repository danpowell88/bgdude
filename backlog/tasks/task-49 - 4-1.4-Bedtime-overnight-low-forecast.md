## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** Overnight lows — dropping too low while asleep — are among the scariest events, and the most preventable with a heads-up before bed. bgdude has only a crude overnight check today, even though it already has all the ingredients (insulin still working, tonight's basal schedule, the day's exercise, alcohol, and your overnight patterns).

**Reason for change.** A proper bedtime forecast that estimates overnight-low risk and suggests a small snack is high daily value. It grades itself each morning against what actually happened so you can see if it's trustworthy.

## Implementation Plan

<!-- SECTION:PLAN:BEGIN -->
- New `insights/overnight_low.dart`: hourly deterministic run to +8h, residual correction to 120 min, then extrapolate the LOWER band edge only (no point-accuracy pretense at 6h).
- Risk = f(min lower edge, minutes <80, aerobic load, alcohol) → 3 tiers with reasons.
- Snack sizing via `rescue_carbs.dart`, advisory-only.
- Deliver a Tonight card after ~20:30 + the `overnightLowRisk` notification (replace the cruder heuristic).
- Self-grading: log tier + actual overnight minimum, reconcile next morning, show hit/miss on the accuracy screen.
- Tests: tiering on synthetic evenings; reconciliation next-morning test; Tonight-card integration test; ML-honesty tests (coverage + bias, synthetic recovery); guard the forecaster promotion gate.
- Verify: `flutter analyze` clean, `flutter test` green.
