## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** bgdude never deletes old records, so prediction logs and health samples pile up forever.

**Reason for change.** Pruning stale predictions (older than 90 days) and health samples (older than 180 days) — while keeping all glucose and insulin history, which is the training corpus — keeps the database lean. Also a prerequisite for the higher-volume 15-minute forecast.
