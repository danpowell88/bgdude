## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** The morning summary doesn't give a single at-a-glance sense of how much low-glucose risk today carries.

**Reason for change.** A daily hypo-risk score (a standard "low blood glucose index" compared to your own two-week baseline) with one plain reason gives a quick morning read on risk.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->

## Acceptance Criteria
<!-- AC:BEGIN -->
- [ ] #1 LBGI computed vs a 14-day baseline
- [ ] #2 Score + one reason string shown in the morning summary
- [ ] #3 Degrades gracefully with <14 days of data
<!-- AC:END -->

## Implementation Plan

<!-- SECTION:PLAN:BEGIN -->
- Compute LBGI vs a 14-day baseline.
- Add the score + one reason string to the morning summary.
- Degrade gracefully with <14 days of data.
- Tests: unit test LBGI + baseline; test the <14-day fallback; morning-summary integration test; add/extend unit tests under `test/`.
- Verify: `flutter analyze` clean, `flutter test` green.
