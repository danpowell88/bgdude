## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** When bgdude forecasts your glucose it draws a shaded "uncertainty band" around the line. Today that band is symmetric — equal room above and below. But real glucose risk is lopsided: depending on the situation you're much more likely to drift one way than the other.

**Reason for change.** A lopsided ("asymmetric") band that's wider on the riskier side is the foundation of an honest forecast and a trustworthy low-alert. This builds it using "quantile" models that predict the 10th/50th/90th-percentile outcomes instead of just the average.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
