## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** Exercise moves glucose strongly and in different directions — steady aerobic exercise (a run) tends to lower it for hours, while intense anaerobic effort (heavy lifting) can push it up. bgdude's forecast currently ignores exercise entirely.

**Reason for change.** Post-exercise lows are common and dangerous, so making the forecast aware of recent workouts improves both its accuracy and how early it can warn of a low.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
