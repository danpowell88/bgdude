## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**Background.** The pump can report exactly which icons it is showing on its own home screen right now — whether basal is running or suspended, whether Control-IQ (the pump's automation) is active, the CGM trend arrow, and status icons. bgdude doesn't surface this yet. These read messages were confirmed to return real data during the July 2026 live-pump exploration (the Protocol Explorer sweep); their exact byte layouts are recorded in doc/pump-protocol.md.

**Reason for change.** A live "what my pump shows right now" panel is the headline feature from the pump exploration — it mirrors the device you already trust, in plain language, and cross-checks that bgdude is decoding the pump correctly.
